How It Works

Our system uses three powerful algorithms: ARIMA (statistical time series forecasting), LSTM (deep learning), and Linear Regression (trend analysis). Together, they provide a comprehensive prediction.

We analyze recent tweets about the stock to gauge market sentiment. This sentiment data is incorporated into our prediction model to account for public perception and market psychology.

Get detailed visualizations of historical data, model predictions, and future forecasts. We also provide a 7-day forecast to help you plan your investment strategy.

About The Project

This stock market prediction application utilizes multiple machine learning algorithms and Twitter sentiment analysis to provide forecasts for stock prices. The three main algorithms used are:

  • ARIMA (Autoregressive Integrated Moving Average) - A statistical analysis model that uses time series data to predict future values.
  • LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory) - A type of recurrent neural network (RNN) capable of learning long-term dependencies, particularly useful for time series prediction.
  • Linear Regression - A linear approach to modeling the relationship between the dependent variable (stock price) and independent variables.

Additionally, we incorporate sentiment analysis from Twitter to gauge market perception. This helps in understanding how public sentiment might influence stock prices.
